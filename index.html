About PharmaCraft

Crafted by an Educator

PharmaCraft is built and curated by Dr. Susheel Kumar, with a focus on clinical integration and exam-oriented teaching.

Our Mission

To make Pharmacology — often considered one of the most volume-heavy subjects in medicine — conceptual, visual and clinically meaningful. Every drug here is explained with mechanism animations, classification trees, side-by-side comparisons and exam-style MCQs so the student remembers how and why a drug works, not just the name.

Who is this for?

  • MBBS Students – revision before professional exams
  • NEET PG / INI-CET Aspirants – high-yield mock practice
  • FMGE Candidates – exam-pattern questions and quick recap
  • MD Pharmacology Residents – structured drug profiles for teaching
